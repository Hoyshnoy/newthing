1954 Mercury Monterey Sport Coupe

This stunning 1954 Mercury Monterey Sport Coupe features its beautiful factory red and white exterior paired with a matching red and white interior. Mercury unleashed major technology upgrades that year, introducing the indestructible Y-block overhead valve engine. The ride, handling, and steering were vastly improved with newly designed tie rods, ball joints, and a smooth-riding front suspension. Mercury’s designers were light years ahead with aerodynamic styling, a low roofline, and a finless sweeping tail end. Inside, you’ll find pilot-in-command T-handled sliding controls and a smaller, sportier steering wheel for a true cockpit feel of comfort and control. This car has had a ground-up restoration that brings it to a new level, including power front disc brakes, Coker radials with the correct whitewall width, and a fully restored radio upgraded with a concealed CD player and front and rear speakers.

  • Beautiful factory red and white exterior with matching interior
  • Indestructible Y-block overhead valve engine
  • Improved ride, handling, and steering with upgraded suspension, tie rods, and ball joints
  • Aerodynamic body styling with low roofline and sweeping tail end
  • Unique interior with pilot-in-command T-handled sliding controls
  • Sportier steering wheel for comfort and control
  • Ground-up restoration with power front disc brakes
  • Equipped with Coker radials and correct whitewall width
  • Radio restoration and concealed CD player with front and rear speakers
